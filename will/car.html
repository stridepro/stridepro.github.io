Let's Fix it Founded by Will Greenlee, certified auto mechanic

Will Greenlee is a lifelong car enthusiast. While completing his auto mechanic certification at Ferris State University, he encountered numerous young car owners with questions about a noise their car was making, unsure if the car needed immediate repair to ensure safety or if the issue was ignorable. After helping more than 100 local individuals, Will Greenlee realized how many other people must have car questions. Thus, Let's Fix It was born. Free car advice through your smartphone, wherever you are, from a certified auto mechanic.

Our Mechanics

Each of our mechanics are rigorously trained by the **National Car Association** standards and must have at least 10 years of auto mechanic experience. Each mechanic has abundant training in all car issues from dirty tires to engine grease.

100% Free

Let's Fix it consultations are 100% free. If you do decide to repair your car, Let's Fix it is partnered with auto-repair shops nationwide that meet our standards. Through these partnerships and referrals, Let's Fix it is able to provide you consultations free of charge.
