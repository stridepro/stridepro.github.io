Free stride analysis

stridePRO is the future of movement analysis research. What normally requires a state-of-the-art laboratory and computers is now available to you, for free, by submitting a sample of your stride.

  1. remove shoes and socks, roll up pants so ankles are visible
  2. hold smartphone at chest level, camera facing down
  3. ensure feet are within the view of the camera
  4. begin recording
  5. look up and walk about 10 steps (as normally as possible)
  6. stop recording once you have finished walking
  7. submit video, include your email address and any problems/pain/concerns you may have under "note" field (e.g. bunion right foot, left foot pain, right broken ankle 2008)
